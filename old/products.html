One EMR. All platforms

Finally, an EMR that you can take with you and automatically change features and functions based on your location/task.

Infinite Customizations means Infinite Possibilities

Making an EMR with infinite customization doesn't just mean getting to choose what colors are on the screen.
It means being able to:

  • Let Doctors keep the charts they've always been using, but on the backend translate what the doctor means on paper into standard format for anyone to read: including administrative professionals, and even other doctors
  • Need to change clinical documentation as a patient encounter progresses? EVA EHR is the first EMR to allow clinical staff to pull up clinical templates at the touch of a button. Making it easier to ensure that no miscommunication arises during treatment planning
  • Auto Send Chart Features
    If a patient needs to be sent to the E.R. or a specialist, EVA allows doctors to quickly point out during the encounter what their reason for sending the patient is, and EVA will update with background information necessary that nearby Hospitals or specialists will want.

Scalability

EVA EMR is designed from the ground up to be an easy to use and efficient tool for clinical and administrative staff to use.
One important design decision was to give our users the ability to scale up Eva's features as they grow. When a single practitioner expands his practice, or a hospital system opens a new facility: EVA EMR offers unique features which give it's users unparalled migration possibilities.
What does that mean? It's designed to quickly accomodate on all scales of users needs.

EVA Cloud.

Not a simple backup. EVA EMR with Eva Cloud is the world's first EMR you can take with you. No matter where your patients are.

For the busiest doctors, they can encounter multiple EMRs depending on where they are practicing. Busy doctors practice in more than one location, and for more than one hospital system. Having EVA Cloud access allows you to deal with one EMR no matter what hospital or practice you are making your rounds in.

Simply upload the blank documentation from each location to EVA cloud, and then pull up the documentation on your phone, tablet, or laptop. Once finished, save, print, and upload. Let EVA do the rest for you.

EVA Insurance API

Do clinicals your way. We'll automate the rest

One of the most common reasons in denials and delays in patient treatment by the insurance companies are key details missing from documentation. EVA Ehr is the world's first EMR to tackle this head on, by offering workflow automation.

We allow clinical staff to continue doing clinicals their own way, but when they are ready to submit their clinical information for treatment authorization, Eva automatically pulls up and directs the doctors submission based on the patient's insurance requirements. That's right. Welcome to the Age of AutoAuthorize.

Care Calls

Contacting the insurance company has never been easier.

No one likes being put on hold, only to have to spend valuable time hunting and finding patient demographics that aren't always readily available. With CareCall, if a clinical or administrative staff has a question, or issue that needs to be addressed with the insurance company: they simply press the carecall button. This button autogenerates a number and can send it directly to the doctors phone, or office VOIP solutions. If neither of those options exist, the care call will automatically display the phone number for the clinical staff to call. Once a representative picks up, the doctor simply has to provide the CareCall number that was generated, and the insurance representative can see exactly what the doctor sees.

Autobill and Autoclaim

Insurances have the ability to allow practices the ability to automatically bill after patient encounters, which will drastically improve reimbursement, and virtually reduce denials and resubmit requests.
